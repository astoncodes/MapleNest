# 🍁 MapleNest

**MapleNest** is a community-focused housing platform built to make renting easier, more transparent, and more accessible for students, young professionals, and local landlords.

The platform helps users discover available rentals, post housing opportunities, and connect directly with others in their community. Whether someone is searching for a long-term apartment, looking to sublease a room, or posting a listing as a landlord, MapleNest is designed to simplify the process and make local housing more trustworthy.

Starting in **Prince Edward Island**, MapleNest is tailored to the needs of **UPEI students, Holland College students, young professionals, and independent landlords**, with a long-term vision of expanding to communities across Canada.

## What MapleNest Does

MapleNest gives renters and landlords a shared platform where they can:

- browse available rental listings in their area
- create and manage housing postings
- post rooms, apartments, or sublease opportunities
- connect directly through built-in messaging
- view clearer pricing and listing details
- build trust through a more transparent housing experience

## Key Features

- **Rental Listings**  
  Users can browse housing options in one place instead of relying on scattered posts across multiple platforms.

- **Create a Posting**  
  Landlords and tenants can post available units, rooms, or shared housing opportunities.

- **Sublease Support**  
  Renters can list subleases for rooms or apartments, making it easier for students and temporary residents to find short-term options.

- **Direct Messaging**  
  Built-in chat allows renters and landlords to communicate directly within the platform.

- **Transparent Listing Details**  
  Listings are designed to present pricing and property information more clearly so users can make informed decisions.

- **Community-Focused Experience**  
  MapleNest is built with local housing needs in mind, starting with PEI communities.
